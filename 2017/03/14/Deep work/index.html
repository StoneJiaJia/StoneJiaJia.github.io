Deep work 笔记

发表于 Mar 14 2017

1 Deep Work 和 Shadow Work

1.1 Deep Work

Professional activities performed in a state if distraction-free that push your cognitive capabilities to their limit. These efforts creat bew value, improve your skill

1.2 Shadow Work

Noncognitively damanding, logistical-style tasks, often performed while distracted. These efforts tend to not creat much value and are easy to replicated

由于现在的世界充斥着网络，导致我们的文化不断向Shadow Work所转变 "Financial Consultant"(理财顾问）这类工作被计算机程序所取代("Klude together" Excel script) Excel 中一个叫做 "macros"的功能能让6小时的工作量只需要按一个回车就能解决。

1.3 Deep Work Hypothesis

it is becoming increasingly valuable in our economy, the few who cultivate this skill,and the make it the core of their working life, will thrive

1.4 High-Skilled Work

data visualization, analytics, high speed communication, rapid prototyping…. More abstract and data-driven reasoning

1.5 Winner-take-all

在新的社会体制下，这三类人占有巨大的优势 Those who can work well and creatively with intelligent machines, those who are the best at what they do those with access to capital

1.6 How to Become a Winner in the New Economy

  1. The ability to quickly master hard things
  2. The ability to produce at an elite level, in terms if both quality and speed

2 刻意练习(deliberate practice)

  1. 你的注意力能集中在你想要增强的能力上
  2. 你能随时收到反馈并且正确把注意力放在最有效率的地方

为了快速学习难的东西的时候，你必须注意力集中，不能分心，所以这种“学习”也是一种"Deep Work"

高质量的工作产出 = 所花的时间 * 注意力集中的程度

当你从任务A转到任务B时,你的注意力不会立即集中在任务B上，(remain stuck thinking about original task) 为了在注意力最高峰的时候有产出，你必须在单一的任务上保持集中，不能有任何分心的事或人。(Deep Work)

3 Deep Work is rare

3.1 The Metric Black Hole

depth-destroying behaviors 摧毁深度工作的行为

4 Deep Work is Meaninngful

4.1 Deep Work 神经科学

我们的大脑对于周围世界的认知基于我们把注意力放在哪里,如果我们把注意力集中在癌症诊断上，生活就会变得不幸福和黑暗 如果你的注意力放在马提尼酒上就会越开心（pleasant)

If you spend enough time in this state(deep work),your mind will understand your world as rich in meaning and importance. 所以培养Deep work的状态很重要，Deep work lead to happer life

现在的许多知识工作者把注意力放在消极的消息上了

4.2 Deep Work 心理学

Deep Work 类似于“心流”的状态。 心流：在有目标，及时反馈，有挑战的任务中，忘记了时间的流逝，自己完全沉浸的状态

To build your working life around experience of flow produced by deep work is a proven path to deep satisfaction

4.3 Deep Work 的哲学

Beautiful code is short and concise, so if you were to give that code to another programmer they say, "Oh, that's well written code." It's much like as if were written a poem

Deep Work 会对所从事的事有种荣誉感和满足感，认为是具有美感的，艺术的。 A deep life is a good life, any way you look at it

11 Embrace Boredom

注意力就像肌肉需要训练

11.1 don't take breaks from distraction, instead take breaks from focus

再禁止自己浏览网页的头三十分钟会很无聊，但是之后会逐渐习惯。渐渐地变成一种Mental Traning（注意力集中练习）

  • 确保自己不浏览任何网站
  • 在限制自己使用互联网的同时在训练自己的专注力
